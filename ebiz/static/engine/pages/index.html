Welcome To The Surveyor's Centre

We are a group of Surveyors dedicated to bring the most services concerning the Geomatic and Gis fields.
At our humble beginings,we had two surveyors who came up with the idea of running a website as is now to offer all the possible Survey Services.These services include:-
*Planning
*Cadastral Survey
*Highway Engineering
*Geodetic Survey(in progress)
*GIS
*Remote Sensing
; We have since grown to being an organization whose overal objectives is to provide quality services to the everyone , so that we can help solve existing issues and provide good plans for the future.
We have two types of management ie : External management constituted of Board of Directors and the Internal management , headed by the Principal and The Deputy Principal.The other offices under the internal management are office of the Dean of students , Assistant Principal Finance , Internal Auditor and the Head of Departments.
The Geomatics Centre is registered by the
Institution of Surveyors of Kenya, under The Survey Act.
We are also in collaboration with other companies to build a powerful network for quicker support to our esteemed customers.
